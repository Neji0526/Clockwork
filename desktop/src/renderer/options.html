ClockWork settings

Dashboard URL

Paste the URL of your ClockWork dashboard (e.g. https://clockwork.yourcompany.com). The "dashboard" link in the popup opens this address.

Sampling

These control how often ClockWork samples while you're clocked in. Defaults are fine for most people.

What's captured

While clocked in: the active website and how long you're on it, idle stretches, a periodic screenshot of the active tab, and the steps you click (used to build SOPs from work you repeat). Nothing is captured when you're clocked out or on a break. You can see all of it on your dashboard — it's the same data your admin sees.
